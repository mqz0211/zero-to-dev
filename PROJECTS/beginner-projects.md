## How to actually get value from these

1. **Don't copy-paste a tutorial's finished code.** Read the goal, close
   the tutorial, and try first. Get stuck on purpose ‚Äî that's where the
   learning happens.
2. **Push every project to GitHub**, even the small ones ‚Äî see the
   [GitHub guide](../CAREERS/github-guide.md).
3. **Add one feature beyond the spec** once it works ‚Äî this is the
   single best habit for actually learning versus just finishing.
